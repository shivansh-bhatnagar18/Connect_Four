Welcome to the Offline Multiplayer Connect Four

Here are the rules of Connect Four:

1) The game starts with an empty game board.

2) Players take turns dropping colored disks into the columns from the top.

3) Once a disk is dropped into a column, it falls to the lowest unoccupied space in that column.

4) The first player to connect four of their colored disks vertically, horizontally, or diagonally wins the game.

5) If all spaces on the board are filled without a winner, then the game is considered a tie.

6) Players cannot remove a disk once it has been dropped.

7) A player must drop their disk into a column that is not already full.

8) Players must connect their disks in a straight line. This means that a diagonal line of three disks and one empty space does not count as a win.

9) The game can be played again and again, with players alternating who goes first
